Heritage Timeline

1947

Ferrari is founded by Enzo Ferrari. The first Ferrari car, the 125 S, is produced.

1962

The legendary Ferrari 250 GTO is released, cementing Ferrari's reputation for excellence.

1987

Ferrari introduces the F40, the last car to be personally approved by Enzo Ferrari.

2024

Ferrari celebrates its 77th anniversary, continuing to innovate in the automotive industry.
